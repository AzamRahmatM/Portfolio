Accessibility Statement

I am committed to ensuring that this website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. My goal is to continually improve the user experience and ensure the site meets accessibility standards.

Steps Taken to Improve Accessibility

  • Ensured the site works across screen readers and other assistive technologies.
  • Designed the site to be fully navigable using a keyboard.
